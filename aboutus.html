About Us

Team 7 consists of Gabriel Mireles, Vanessa Ramirez, and Fernando Davila. We chose this topic for our sustainability project as we felt that environmental sustainability prevails over all else when it comes to the effect it could have on social and economic sustainability. Green cloud computing is something that is a simple answer to questions of what actions are best for a company to future proof itself in all factors of its business.
